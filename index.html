About this tool

In plain terms: this is a practice quiz for judging AI-style answers the way teams do when they train helpful models. You either pick the better reply to a question, or read a tiny “what went wrong” story (a few log lines) and type what you think broke. The app scores you, tracks how you’re doing, and can download a report when you’re done. Nothing leaves your browser unless you export it yourself.
